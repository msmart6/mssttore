Logistics & Shipment

Safe, Secure, and Timely Delivery Worldwide

Sea Cargo

We provide reliable sea freight services for bulk orders. Our strong partnerships with leading shipping lines ensure your goods reach any port in the world safely and on time.

Container Sizes: 20ft (approx 28 Tons) & 40ft High Cube.

Load Type: FCL (Full Container Load) & LCL (Less Container Load).

Handling: Temperature controlled reefer containers for Vegetables.

Air Cargo

For urgent shipments and highly perishable items, our Air Cargo service is the best choice. We ensure flight connectivity from Hyderabad and Chennai airports within 24-48 hours.

Weight Limit: Minimum 100 Kgs to 5+ Tons per flight.

Best For: Fresh Vegetables, Leafy Greens, and Samples.

Customs: Express clearance for perishable goods.

On Road Transport

We have a robust domestic logistics network to transport goods from our farms and warehouses to the air/sea ports securely. Our fleet ensures minimal transit damage.

Vehicles: 10 Ton, 14 Ton, and 25 Ton Trucks.

Facilities: GPS Tracking & Tarpaulin Covered / Closed Containers.

Connectivity: Direct farm pickup to Port/Airport drop.
