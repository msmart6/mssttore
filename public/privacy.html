Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we handle your data.

At MS MART (Eluru, Andhra Pradesh), we value the trust you place in us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information in connection with your use of our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

When you visit MS MART, we collect certain information to process your orders efficiently:

  • Personal Information: Name, Email Address, Phone Number, and Shipping Address.
  • Order Details: Products purchased, quantities, and transaction history.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information to improve site performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To process and deliver your orders (Domestic & International).
  • To communicate with you regarding order updates, tracking, and customer support.
  • To prevent fraud and ensure the security of our platform.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal information to third parties. We share data only with:

  • Logistics Partners: Courier services and delivery personnel to ensure your package reaches you.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or government authorities for compliance purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement strict security measures, including Google Firebase Authentication and secure servers, to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your shopping experience, remember your cart items, and understand user behavior. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you. You may also request corrections or deletion of your data by contacting our support team.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@msmart.com
Address: MS MART, Eluru, Andhra Pradesh, India.
